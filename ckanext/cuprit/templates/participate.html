There are two ways to submit deposits.

As a DAI employee

As a DAI staff member, log in to the system with your known username and password. Click on "Add Dataset" and fill in all required metadata fields. Finally, upload your files.

After saving, an administrator will receive an e-mail, complete your metadata if necessary and publish the deposit.

an external person

As an external person, please download the following PDF form new-dataset.pdf . Save it filled out together with all your files to be deposited in a folder. Package your folder as a "zip" archive (zip with windows) and send it to : new-dataset@dainst.org.

If your archive is too large to be sent by email, please use a file transfer service such as Dropbox, Cumulus, https://wetransfer.com, https://www.sendbig.com. If this is not possible. You can also send us FTP access data. If no server is available we can provide FTP access.

After receiving the data, an administrator will add your metadata if necessary and publish the deposit.
