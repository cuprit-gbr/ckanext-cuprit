Submit data as a DAI Employee

As a DAI staff member:

  1. log in to the system with your known username and password
  2. Click on "Add Dataset"
  3. fill in all required metadata fields
  4. Finally, upload your files.

After saving, an administrator will receive an e-mail, complete your metadata if necessary and publish the deposit.

Submit data as an external Person

As an external person

  1. download the following PDF form new-dataset.pdf
  2. Save it filled out together with all your files to be deposited in a dedicated folder.
  3. Package your folder as a "zip" archive (zip with windows)
  4. send it to : new-dataset@dainst.org

If your archive is too large to be sent by email, please use a file transfer service such as

  • Dropbox
  • Cumulus
  • Wetransfer
  • Sendbig
If this is not possible. You can also send us FTP access data. If no FTP-server is available for you we can provide FTP access from our side.

After receiving the data, an administrator will add your metadata if necessary and publish the deposit.
